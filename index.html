About Us

FeatherStone is a quality product from the house of Stone Craft International.

  • 1945

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Stone Craft International is a part of 60 years old business group, fondly known as the hub of Stone in India. We commenced our operations in 1945 on very modest footing and grown into multi-million rupee empire with diversified interests in stones, hardware, automobiles, agriculture implements etc.

  • 2004

    Stonecraft International

    With strong financial backing created within the family businesses, our overseas networks and the goodwill earned in the domestic market of stones in last two decades, it was possible to launch the EOU company in 2004 in the name and style of STONE CRAFT INTERNATIONAL. Currently the Company is engaged in quarrying, manufacturing and export of multi-product range that includes Indian Sand Stones, Lime Stones, Slates, etc with wide range of colors, finishes and textures. The Company has its state-of-the-art Plants in the sandstone belt of Rajasthan, at Kota.

  • Our Philosophy

    The true assets of any organization are its people. We have a team of dedicated and skilled workers. Our organizational culture encourages the quest for excellence and a progressive and futuristic attitude

  • Quality

    Stone Craft International is like an artist sitting with a palette of colors, Superlative Indian Colors! Latest machineries, highly trained staff, strict quality control and customer orientation has ensured a satisfied customer-base. Our raw materials, block cutters, an accurate calibrating system and well maintained polishing & bevelling makes a difference to the Stone Craft International quality.
