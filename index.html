Feather Stone



Feather stone is a lightweight, flexible, colorful and unique stone veneer consists of a thin layer of stone.Being durable, flexible & weather resistant it has endless application possibilities. It can be used almost anywhere for Exterior or Interior purposes. May it be on Exterior Facades, Ceilings, Furniture, Elevators, Bath & Shower wall, Backdrops or Counters, with its capability to bend & take shape of curves, the applications are innumerable.

Features:

  • Light Weight
  • Fast Installation
  • High Strength
  • High Impact Resistance
  • Durable
  • No Health Risk
  • Easy Fixing

Specification

  • Weight-1.60-1.88 Kg per Sqm

  • Material thickness: 2mm-3mm

  • Sheet Size-Upto 2400 x 1200 mm
